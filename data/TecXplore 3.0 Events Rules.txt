Event Name: The “Traitors” Game
Synopsis:
The Traitors – City Mystery Game is a strategic, role-based deduction game inspired by classic social
deception formats. Participants are secretly assigned roles as Killers, Doctors, or Citizens, while a Speaker
(volunteer) moderates the game. The game revolves around night and day phases, where hidden actions and
open discussions determine the survival of players. The game encourages logical thinking, communication,
teamwork, and strategic decision-making.

Objective:
The objective of the game is to identify and eliminate all the Killers through discussion and voting, while
Killers aim to eliminate all other participants without being identified. Doctors attempt to protect players
and prevent eliminations during the night phase.

Roles Involved:
• Killers: 3–4 participants
• Doctors: 1–2 participants
• Citizens: Remaining participants
• Speaker: One volunteer who controls the flow of the game

Rules and Regulations:

• All roles are assigned secretly at the beginning of the game.
• The Speaker will announce different phases of the game and participants must act accordingly.

Night Phase:
• When the Speaker announces, “City Sleep,” all participants must close their eyes.
• The Speaker will then announce, “Killers Awake.”
• All Killers will silently open their eyes and, through mutual signals, select one
participant to eliminate.
• After the Killers close their eyes, the Speaker will announce “Doctors Awake.”
• Doctors will silently select one participant to save.

• The selected participant by the Doctors may be anyone, including a Killer or the target
chosen by the Killers.

Day Phase:
• The Speaker will announce “City Awake,” and all participants open their eyes.
• The Speaker will reveal if a participant has been eliminated during the night (unless
saved by the Doctors).
• All participants are given exactly two minutes to discuss and analyze who they believe
the Killers are.
• After discussion, participants must vote for one suspected Killer.

Elimination Rules:
• The participant targeted by the Killers will be eliminated unless saved by the Doctors.
• The participant who receives the highest number of votes during the day phase
will also be eliminated unless saved by the Doctors.
• Eliminated participants must exit the game silently and are not allowed to reveal their
roles.

Winning Conditions:
• Citizens & Doctors win if all Killers are eliminated.
• Killers win if they outnumber the remaining participants.

General Guidelines:
• No communication is allowed during the night phase.
• Participants must always follow the Speaker’s instructions.
• Any form of cheating, signaling outside assigned phases, or role disclosure will
result in immediate disqualification.
Registration Fee: ₹30 per person
